Leaders in Biomedial Science Joining Forces

The Association of American Physicians (AAP)

Founded in 1885, the AAP has over 1,900 elected members with shared goals that include the pursuit of medical knowledge, and the advancement through experimentation and discovery of basic and clinical science and their application to clinical medicine.

aap-online.org

The American Society for Clinical Investigation (ASCI)

Founded in 1885, the AAP has over 1,900 elected members with shared goals that include the pursuit of medical knowledge, and the advancement through experimentation and discovery of basic and clinical science and their application to clinical medicine.

the-asci.org

The American Physician Scientists Association (APSA)

Founded in 2003, APSA is the largest peer support community for physician scientist trainees, representing more than 1,700 members. APSA strives to be the student physician-scientists’ leading voice for improving educational opportunities, advancing patient-oriented research, and advocating for the future of science and medicine.

physicianscientists.org
